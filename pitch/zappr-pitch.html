the moment
Origin story

A friend, a phone, and forty wasted minutes.

"She finally got the payment to land,
looked up at me, and said —
'this is supposed to be the future?'"

A small-business owner in Lagos was paid in Bitcoin for the first time. It took three apps, a YouTube tutorial, and a WhatsApp guide who kept sending links she didn't understand. The payment worked. The experience didn't.

That sentence is why zappr exists.

02 / 10
the problem
What's broken

Bitcoin works. The experience doesn't.

Fragmented

Wallet in one app. Social in another. Help in a third. Three logins, three contexts, zero memory between them.

English-only

Every Bitcoin tool ships in English first. A Yoruba trader has to think in a second language to send money.

Jargon wall

Lightning. NWC. zaps. nsec. The vocabulary alone filters out 95% of would-be users.

False choice

"Easy" apps custody your keys. Sovereign apps are too technical. Nothing is both friendly and self-custodial.

how it works
Architecture

Sovereign by default. Friendly by design.

Nostr · NDK

Your identity, your follows, your relays. We never see your nsec.

Lightning · NWC

Bring any wallet — Alby, Mutiny, Primal. We just hold the connection.

Claude Agent

Reads, drafts, explains. Multilingual. Confirms before any payment.

The trust model

Keys live in your browser. Biometric vault (WebAuthn PRF + AES-GCM) means no re-typing nsecs. Logout wipes everything. No server. No custody. No excuses.
